About Us

At Alberto Pichardo Tennis Pro Tennis School, we are passionate about helping players of all ages and skill levels improve their game. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced coaches make learning tennis fun and effective.

Founder of the school, Alberto Pichardo has over 30 years of experience coaching professional and amateur tennis players. He has been the Head Tennis Pro at Sports Illustrated Resorts Marina & Villas Cap Cana for many years, teaching many players in different levels.
